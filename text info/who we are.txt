Bike King Borders  was established in 2017 and offer professional bike hire, bike servicing and bike accessories here in the Borders. We are a family run business and are proud of the service which we provide. We offer a range of adult bikes including Mountain, Hybrid and Electric Bikes. 

We also have children's bikes from 7 years upwards plus Tag-Alongs, Toddler Trailers and Baby Seats. All of which are free of charge when hired with adult bikes.

Our bikes are maintained to the highest standards and serviced before every hire. We are based close to Castle Douglas which is close to many of the borders internationally renowned bike trails.

We are covered by full public liability insurance and are registered with the chamber of commerce.
